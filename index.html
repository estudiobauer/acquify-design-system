06 / Sections
01 / Principles

Five rules that govern everything.

The brand is predominantly dark. Light emerges from within, never imposed from outside. These five principles precede every design decision.

01
Dark base
Light emerges from within the composition, not laid on top of it.
02
Organic structure
No mechanical grids. Form follows breath, not measurement.
03
Selective luminosity
Brightness is earned. Reserve light for what matters most.
04
Volume and depth
Flat is not Acquify. Every surface has weight and atmosphere.
05
Monochromatic range
Black to off-white. No chromatic accents. Ever.
